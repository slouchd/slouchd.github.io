Today I Learned [or Remembered]!

As an engineer with nearly a decade of experience in the industry. I have learned a lot over the years. Every day brings new challenges and opportunities to grow, and I am always striving to improve my skills and knowledge.

But I have also realized that with so much information to absorb, it is easy to forget some of the important lessons I have learned along the way. That is why I have started keeping a daily log of the things I have learned and remembered. Or in alot of cases re-remembered!

My goal is to share these insights with others who may be on a similar journey of constant learning and growth. I want to provide value by offering practical tips and advice that can help fellow engineers succeed in their careers.

Through my writing, I aim to use a clear and concise style that is easy to read and understand. Hopefully my musings will help you remember or learn something too.
